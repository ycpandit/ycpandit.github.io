Hello! I'm Frodo Baggins. I'm a 3rd PhD student in Computer Science at Chennai Mathematical Institute. I'm working under the supervision of Prof. Thorin Oakenshield.

I am broadly interested in Complexity Theory and Sublinear Algorithms I like working on anything that involves polynomials and second eigenvalue.

Before this, I did my masters from Hogwarts, and my bachelors in information technology from the Durmstrang Institute, North Europe.
